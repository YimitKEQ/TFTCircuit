Frequently Asked Questions

Is coaching helpful for lower-ranked players?

Absolutely. Coaching is beneficial for players of all skill levels, as it provides personalized feedback and strategies that you might not find in general guides.

What should I bring to a session?

It's helpful to have a specific goal in mind, such as improving your early game, learning a new composition, or reviewing a recent VOD. Come prepared with questions!

How are coaches vetted?

All coaches are required to submit proof of their peak rank and coaching experience. We also review their content and community feedback to ensure high-quality instruction.
