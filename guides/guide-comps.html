Set 15 Compositions Guide

Coach: KEQING/Levitate 25 min watch

This guide provides an overview of the top competitive compositions for K.O. Coliseum, complete with positioning and itemization recommendations. The meta is constantly evolving, so be sure to check back for updates.

S-Tier Comps

These are the compositions that are currently dominating the meta. They are the most consistent and have the highest win rates.

Darius Reroll

This composition focuses on slow rolling for a 3-star Darius, who becomes an unstoppable force with the right items. The core of this composition is the Juggernaut trait, which provides Darius with a massive amount of survivability.

  • Core Champions: Darius, Garen, Aatrox, Dr. Mundo, Shen, Yasuo, Sett, Yone
  • Itemization: Bloodthirster, Titan's Resolve, and Infinity Edge on Darius.
  • Strategy: Slow roll at level 7 to find Darius 3. Once you have Darius 3, push to level 8 and add in legendary units.

Kog'Maw Reroll

This composition utilizes the Sniper and Monster Trainer traits to create a powerful backline carry in Kog'Maw. With the right items, Kog'Maw can melt through even the tankiest of frontlines.

  • Core Champions: Kog'Maw, Caitlyn, Gnar, Malphite, Shen, Janna, Ashe, Braum
  • Itemization: Guinsoo's Rageblade, Statikk Shiv, and Runaan's Hurricane on Kog'Maw.
  • Strategy: Slow roll at level 7 to find Kog'Maw 3. Position Kog'Maw in the corner and protect him with a strong frontline.

A-Tier Comps

A-Tier compositions are strong and can win lobbies, but they may be more situational or harder to execute than S-Tier comps.

Battle Academia

This composition uses the Battle Academia trait to create a powerful and flexible team. The key is to itemize your carries correctly and adapt your board to what your opponents are playing.

  • Core Champions: Garen, Ezreal, Caitlyn, Leona, Wukong, Yone, Sett, Irelia
  • Itemization: Varies depending on your carry. Can be AD or AP focused.
  • Strategy: A standard leveling strategy is best for this composition. Push to level 8 and roll for your 4-cost carries.

Wraith Duelists

This composition combines the high-damage output of the Duelist trait with the survivability of the Wraith trait. It's a high-risk, high-reward composition that can be very effective if you can find the right units and items.

  • Core Champions: Kayle, Aatrox, Shen, Riven, K'Sante, Irelia, Yone, Gwen
  • Itemization: Guinsoo's Rageblade and Rapid Firecannon on Kayle.
  • Strategy: This composition can be played as a reroll comp for Kayle or as a standard comp focusing on 4 and 5-cost units.

B-Tier Comps

B-Tier compositions are viable but may struggle against the top-tier comps. They are often good situational choices when you have the right items or augments.

Crystal Gambit Ashe

This composition uses the Crystal Gambit trait to create a powerful late-game carry in Ashe. It's a risky composition that requires a strong early and mid game to pull off, but it can be very rewarding if you can hit your units.

  • Core Champions: Ashe, Swain, Janna, Garen, Shen, Leona, Braum, Irelia
  • Itemization: Guinsoo's Rageblade, Last Whisper, and Infinity Edge on Ashe.
  • Strategy: This is a late-game composition that requires you to be level 8 or 9 to be effective. Focus on building a strong economy in the early and mid game.

Conclusion

This guide has provided a snapshot of the Set 15 meta. Remember that the meta is always evolving, so be sure to stay up-to-date with the latest trends and be prepared to adapt your strategies. Good luck in the K.O. Coliseum!
